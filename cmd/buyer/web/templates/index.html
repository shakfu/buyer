Purchasing Management

Welcome to Buyer - Your purchasing support and vendor quote management tool

Features

Use the sidebar navigation to access different sections of the application:

Master Data

  • Specifications: Define general product types and categories
  • Brands: Track manufacturing brands
  • Products: Organize products by brand and specification
  • Vendors: Manage vendors with multi-currency support

Purchasing Operations

  • Projects: Manage projects with Bill of Materials and linked requisitions
  • Requisitions: Create and track purchasing requirements with budgets
  • Quotes: Track vendor quotes with expiration dates
  • Compare Quotes: Analyze quotes by specification for requisitions, calculate total costs and savings

Configuration

  • Forex Rates: Manage currency exchange rates for automatic price conversion to USD

Quick Stats

Navigate to specific sections to view and manage your purchasing data.
